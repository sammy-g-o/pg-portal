About Crescent University, Abeokuta (CUAB)

Crescent University, Abeokuta (CUAB) is a leading institution of higher learning in Nigeria, dedicated to academic excellence, research, and character development. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programmes across various disciplines.

  • Founded: 2005
  • Accreditation: National Universities Commission (NUC)
  • Core Values: Integrity, Excellence, Innovation, Service

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

The mission of the University is to evolve a unique element for the delivery of university education infused with total quality assurance in ways that will develop personal discipline and integrity and promote positive societal values.

Vision

To breed global citizens rather than “brilliant beats”, as well as to nurture girl child education as majority of females especially Muslims were at a disadvantage.

About CUAB Postgraduate School

The CUAB Postgraduate School is committed to providing advanced education and research opportunities for graduates. Our programmes are designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills required for professional and academic success.

  • Wide range of PGD, Masters, and PhD programmes
  • Experienced faculty and modern facilities
  • Flexible study options (full-time & part-time)
  • Vibrant research community
